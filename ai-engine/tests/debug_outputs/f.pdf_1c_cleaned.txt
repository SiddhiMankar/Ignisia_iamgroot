E] Numerical + Step-Based
1. Speed
Given: Distance = 60 km, Time = 2 hours
Ideal Answer:
• Formula: Speed = Distance / Time
• Substitution: 60 / 2 = 30
• Final Answer: 30 km/h
2. Area of Circle
Given: r = 7 cm
Ideal Answer:
• Formula: Area = πr²
• Substitution: (22/7) × 7 × 7 = 154
• inal Answer: 154 cm²
3. Simple Interest
Given: P = 1000, R = 5%, T = 2
Ideal Answer:
• Formula: SI = (P × R × T) / 100
• Substitution: (1000 × 5 × 2) / 100 = 100
• Final Answer: ₹100
4. Linear Equation
Given: 2x + 5 = 15
Ideal Answer:
• Step 1: 2x = 15 − 5 = 10
• Step 2: x = 10 / 2 = 5
• Final Answer: x = 5
5. Percentage
Given: 45 out of 60
Ideal Answer:
• Formula: Percentage = (Marks / Total) × 100
• Substitution: (45 / 60) × 100 = 75
• Final Answer: 75
